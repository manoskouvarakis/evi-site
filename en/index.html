About Dr. Kosti

Dr. Evi Kosti is a board-certified Pediatrician and Neonatologist with specialized training in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU). She graduated from the University of Athens Medical School, where she also earned a Master's degree in Specialized Pediatrics and Research. She completed her Pediatrics residency at "Agia Sofia" Children's Hospital and went on to sub-specialize in Neonatology at the same institution. During her postgraduate studies, she worked in the Obesity Clinic at "Agia Sofia" Children's Hospital, gaining valuable experience in managing childhood obesity. She has extensive experience as an Intensivist in both the Pediatric and Pediatric Cardiac Surgery ICU at "MITERA" Hospital. She currently practices at her private office and at the Emergency Department of "MITERA" Hospital.

The Office
